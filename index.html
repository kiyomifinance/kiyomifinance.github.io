Kiyomi Finance

Introduction

Kiyomi Finance have a new way to distribute tokens and cross-chain liquidity.

Deflationary Farming Mechanics:

  • Max generate 40,000 POKI
  • 10% tax on transfer:
    • 1% burn
    • 4% for farming
    • 5% for POKICR staking
  • Fixed Emission:
    • 0.11 POKI/block for 10 days
  • Variable Emission:
    • farm received / 10 days

KiCredit System:

  • burn POKI get POKICR
  • POKICR staker get 5% from tax

KiBridge:

  • Support cross-chain liquidity

Farm:

  • Support cross-chain liquidity farming
